About Us

BizInnovate Accelerator is dedicated to helping small and medium-sized businesses adopt AI technologies.

  • Our Mission

    Our mission is to empower businesses through AI and bridge the unemployment gap by equipping young females from disadvantaged backgrounds with essential technical skills.
    We focus on creating opportunities for underrepresented groups to ensure a more inclusive tech landscape.

  • Values

    Passionate dedication to social impact, equality, andinclusivity.

  • Our Approach

    We host tech awareness events, raise awareness and provide education on the transformation power of technology, with a focus on reaching undeserved populations.
