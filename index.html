About Us

BiziNovate is dedicated to empowering females, youth, and small businesses from disadvantaged communities through AI, technology education, IT solutions, and business support.

Our key goals include:
Bridging the digital divide by providing technology and AI education, Offering IT solutions and career opportunities, Fostering community involvement and support for educational initiatives, Emphasizing our commitment to social impact, especially in undeserved populations, particularly women and youth.
